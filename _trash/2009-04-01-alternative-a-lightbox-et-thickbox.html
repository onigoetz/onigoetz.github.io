But a newcomer gives an innovative solution. With the idea that many sites already have a lightbox, why not make them a standard ? Drew Wilson proposes to add the "Fullsize" attribute to the img tag. After all, it's not a bad idea, because it could be used by a bunch of browsers, RSS Readers or other mobile phones. The Developper invites people to sign the petition on his own site. And better, there is a jQuery plugin already available! But because the "fullsize" attribute doesn't exist, it uses the "longdesc" attribute.
